About us

MEDICA RECRUIT

We are an international recruitment agency and specializes in the recruitment of specialist doctors abroad. We have made it our mission to provide our partner clinics with highly qualified medical staff. When it comes to recruitment, we have become the first point of contact for German hospitals.

Our mission is to hire the best candidates for our customers while finding the best jobs for our candidates at the same time. For this reason is very important to know you better to find out about your wishes and preferences regarding new positions. This is the basis for the recruitment process and will support you until you sign the new contract. Once you start working we will advise, support and help you with all official formalities. Also, your social integration is very important to us: we help you start your new life in your new cultural environment so you can feel at home. In our opinion, recruitment is only successful if you and the new employer are satisfied.
